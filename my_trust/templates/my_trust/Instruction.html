This is a trust game. You are paired with another player to play it. The system randomly assigned the roles for each of you.

One of you will be player A and be given {{ Constants.endowment }} as the initial endowment. Player A decides how much of the resources will be transferred to player A.

The amount not transferred will remain as A's but will not grow in value. The resources transferred to B will increase its value by a multiplier of {{Constants.multiplication_factor}}. That is, each point transferred will become {{Constants.multiplication_factor}} points.

Then, player B decides how much of the resources in his/her hands will be given back to player A.

After player B makes the decision, the game ends.
